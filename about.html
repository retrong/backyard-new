About Us

We've Been Thriving in consultancy and software development

Backyard is a consultancy that builds software within structured governance environments. We specialise in supporting organisations operating under regulatory oversight, public accountability, and compliance obligations.

Our work sits at the intersection of strategic advisory and controlled system delivery. We do not separate strategy from execution. We design with implementation in mind and deliver with governance embedded.

Our focus is institutional resilience — ensuring digital systems are secure, auditable, and operationally sustainable.

Our History

Backyard was established to bridge the gap between digital strategy and execution in regulated environments, combining governance-led advisory with structured software delivery.

Our Mission

To enable governments and regulated organisations to deliver secure, compliant, and sustainable digital systems through structured advisory, governance-led design, and controlled software delivery.

Who we are

Backyard is a governance-led technology consultancy that designs and delivers secure digital systems for governments and regulated organisations.

Why we exist

Digital transformation initiatives often fail not because of technology limitations, but because of weak governance alignment, fragmented execution, and insufficient accountability structures. Backyard was established to address this gap.

We exist to ensure that transformation initiatives are:

  • Architecturally sound
  • Policy-aligned
  • Compliance-ready
  • Operationally sustainable
